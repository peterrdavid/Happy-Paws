meet happy paws.

HappyPaws is built around two basic beliefs: all dogs are unique and all dogs deserve to be healthy and happy. As a part of HappyPaws we are able to utilize years of collected product feedback and user testing to make sure that everything in the shop is safe for you and your pooch. Have a question for us or want to chat? Bark at us at happy@paw.com and we’ll be glad to help!

Our promise is to make your dog happy, no matter what, no questions asked.

This is a judgement-free zone for Crazy Dog People. It's not weird to fixate on finding the best for your dog; we're right there with you. Let's work together to find your dog's next favorite toy or snack. Zoomies or bust.

100% Tested on Animals

At HappyPaws we never send out anything that hasn’t been thoroughly tested by ourselves and our pups. We test everything in our offices in NY, NY and send samples to friends around the country with dogs of all types and sizes to get real world user feedback. If it’s in the shop you can trust that it’s been certified safe for your dog.

Pawsonal Shopping

We know some dogs are easier to shop for than others. If you have a finicky Field Spaniel or an allergic Alsatian we’re here to help. Just contact any of our Happy Team representatives and we’ll gladly work with you to find just the right toy or treat for your dog.
